MURAL is a digital workspace for visual collaboration

What we do with Mural

In order to facilitate games online, you may need to use a digital workspace to allow players to share a playful experience with stickies, images and more.

Mural is a great tool to facilitate workshops online. At #play14, we use it to create and facilitate games. For example, we used it for our very first online event.

Many thanks to Mural

Thanks to their Consultant Network offering, Mural gave us the opportunity to use their platform, with up to 10 free member licenses and 25 free guest licenses.

#play14 being a non-profit this is very useful for us, and we are very grateful to Mural for letting us be part of their Consultant Network.
